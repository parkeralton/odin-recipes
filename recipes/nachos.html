Nachos

Description

This is one of my favorite dishes, and one that is easy to make! It's only a few ingredients and will make you want to come back for more cheesy goodness

Ingredients

  • Tortilla Chips
  • Cheese

Directions

  1. Place a layer of chips on a microwave safe plate.
  2. Sprinkle cheese on top of this layer. You should place enough cheese to almost cover the chips completely
  3. Add another layer of chips on top of the cheese. This layer should be smaller than the first layer.
  4. Cover these chips with more cheese. Repeat this process until your heap of nachos is big enough for your appetite.
  5. Microwave in 30 second intervals. Check continuously and stop microwaving when all the cheese has started to melt.

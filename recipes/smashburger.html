Smash Burger

Description

The fastest and tastiest way to make a burger! You'll surprise yourself with how good it tastes.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb Ground Beef
  • 1 Slice Cheese
  • 1 Hamburger Bun
  • Ketchup and Mayo
  • Salt and Pepper

Directions

  1. Get a pan hot over medium high heat. Roll beef into a ball.
  2. Place beef ball on pan. Smash with spatchula and flatten out, you want broken edges!
  3. Season patty with salt and pepper. When edges start to brown, flip patty
  4. Season other side of patty with salt and pepper. Top with slice of cheese. Once fully cooked, remove from pan and place on bun.
  5. Top patty with ketchup and mayo. Enjoy!
